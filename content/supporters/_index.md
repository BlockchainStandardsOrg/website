## Become a Supporter

We welcome blockchain projects, infrastructure providers, and organizations to join our community and support the initiative.

### For Blockchain Projects

Adopt BlockchainStandards.dev specifications in your deployment documentation:

- Implement standardized configuration formats
- Follow packaging and release best practices
- Provide compliant deployment tooling
- Apply for standards certification

[Get Started](/standards/) | [Contact Us](mailto:info@blockchainstandards.dev)

### For Infrastructure Providers

Build services and tooling that support standards-compliant blockchain deployments:

- Develop deployment templates and automation
- Offer managed services following our standards
- Contribute infrastructure best practices
- Showcase your support

[Learn More](/about/) | [Partner With Us](mailto:info@blockchainstandards.dev)

### For Organizations

Support the initiative through sponsorship, contributions, or advocacy:

- Financial sponsorship
- Technical contributions
- Community building
- Standards development

[Contribute](/community/) | [Contact Us](mailto:info@blockchainstandards.dev)

## Recognition Levels

### Platinum Supporters

Organizations providing significant financial support and active participation in standards development.

### Gold Supporters

Blockchain projects and companies fully implementing and certified against our standards.

### Silver Supporters

Organizations actively working toward standards implementation and adoption.

### Community Contributors

Projects and individuals making meaningful contributions to standards development.
