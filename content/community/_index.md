## Join the Community

BlockchainStandards.dev is an open, community-driven initiative. We welcome contributions, feedback, and participation from blockchain developers, node operators, and infrastructure teams worldwide.

---

## Contributing

We welcome contributions in many forms:

- **Standards Development** - Help refine and expand the standards documentation
- **Code Examples** - Share deployment configurations, scripts, and automation tools
- **Community Support** - Answer questions and help other community members
- **Documentation** - Improve guides, tutorials, and reference materials

All contributions are made under our open source license. See our GitHub repository for contribution guidelines.

---

## Community Guidelines

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone. We ask all community members to:

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions
- Provide constructive feedback and suggestions
- Help others learn and grow
- Assume good intentions
